Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

finance-autolog is a personal, non-commercial application used by a single individual to read their own bank account transactions for private expense tracking.

What data is accessed

The application accesses, through a licensed Open Banking (PSD2) account information provider, the transaction history of bank accounts owned by the application's sole operator, who has explicitly granted consent. No other person's data is accessed.

How data is used

Transaction data is used solely to record the operator's own expenses. It is not sold, shared, transferred, or disclosed to any third party.

Storage

Data is stored privately on the operator's own server, secured with standard file permissions. Access is read-only; the application cannot move funds or initiate payments.

Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for personal expense tracking and may be deleted at any time by the operator.
